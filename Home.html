AMBASSADOR's MARCHING BAND

The phenomenal ESTM Ambassadors Marching Band secured first place at the Bandfest 2023, held in the vibrant district of orange-Walk on April 1, 2023. This outstanding achievement is a testament to the incredible talent and dedication of our students and team. They represented ESTM with brilliance and showcased the true spirit of excellence and pride. A special round of applause goes to our extraordinary band director, Mr. Carey McCalla, and his assistant director, Mr. Omir Charley, whose relentless dedication and passion were instrumental in this victory. We also extend heartfelt thanks to Mr. Omir Charley for his expert direction of the E.S.T.M.M.B. on the day of the event.

E.S.T.M.'s FOOTBALL TEAM

The dynamic young football stars from Escuela Secundaria Técnica México have triumphed, winning first place in the national competition! A huge congratulation to our talented students and the entire team for not only representing ESTM with excellence but also showcasing the remarkable talent and spirit of Belize. Your dedication, teamwork, and hard work have truly shone through, earning you this well-deserved victory. A special shout out goes to our incredible coach, Mr. Eric Marin, for his unwavering support and multifaceted assistance to the football team. His strategic guidance, mentorship, and commitment have been key to our success. This achievement is a testament to the passion and perseverance of everyone involved. You’ve made us all incredibly proud, and we can’t wait to see what you accomplish next.
