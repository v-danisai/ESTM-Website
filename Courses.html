FIRST FORM

First Year


The First Form at ESTM, where the journey of secondary education begins! At ESTM, the First Form is a pivotal year designed to bridge the gap between primary education and the more rigorous demands of secondary school. Our comprehensive curriculum introduces students to a wide array of subjects, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning. With a focus on holistic development, we provide a supportive and nurturing environment that encourages academic excellence and personal growth. Our dedicated teachers are committed to guiding each student through this important transition, ensuring they build a strong foundation for future success. Join us in the First Form at ESTM, where every student's potential is nurtured and celebrated.

SECOND FORM

Second Year


The Second Form at ESTM, where students continue to build on the solid foundation established in their first year of secondary education. In the Second Form, the curriculum becomes more challenging and specialized, encouraging students to dive deeper into their academic subjects and discover their unique interests and strengths. Our dedicated and experienced teachers provide personalized guidance and support, ensuring that each student is engaged and motivated to achieve their best. Alongside academic growth, we emphasize the development of essential life skills, such as critical thinking, collaboration, and resilience. At ESTM, the Second Form is a time of exploration and growth, preparing students for the more advanced stages of their educational journey. Join us in the Second Form, where we inspire students to reach new heights and realize their full potential.
